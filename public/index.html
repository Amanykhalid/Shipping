WE PROVIDE SUSTAINABLE INTERVENTIONS FOR

EMPOWERING SOCIAL GROUPS

Welcome at REFORM – The Palestinian Association for Empowerment and Local Development. We are committed to provide sustainable interventions for empowering social groups within the Palestinian Society. We seek to contribute to the formation of a fairer society where women and youth take leading roles in public life and governing bodies.

INITIATING CHANGE

REFORM’s Social Transformation program teaches participants and community members to deal with differences. The participants learn to at least see the value of listening to voices that do not necessarily represent their own views. This leads to increased social cohesion and solidarity between the different Palestinian social components.
